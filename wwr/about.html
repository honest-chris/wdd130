Oseghale Rafting Company

At Oseghale Rafting Company, our purpose is to create safe, exciting, and unforgettable river adventures. Our trained guides, quality gear, and respect for the river help every guest enjoy whitewater rafting with confidence.

History

Oseghale Rafting Company started with a small group of river enthusiasts who loved sharing local rapids with friends and family. Over time, those simple outings grew into guided trips, training programs, and custom adventures for schools, families, and community groups.

We are family owned and operated business dedicated to giving you the best experience possible with experienced guides and quality guide.
